About Me

Academic Profile

Zeydan Çalışkan was born on November 15, 2000, in Turkey, and currently resides in Nicosia, TRNC.

Pursuing his undergraduate studies in Software Engineering at Cyprus International University, he conducts practical projects in cyber security, network analysis, and penetration testing while simultaneously researching emotional intelligence, human-computer interaction, and cultural psychology. His primary goal is to not only build the hardware and network security side of systems, but also contribute scientifically to a human-centered ecosystem by considering the cognitive and emotional processes of individuals.

In addition to advancing his skills in network security and privacy fundamentals, he focuses on multidisciplinary projects where the strict rules of software are synthesized with sociology and psychology.
